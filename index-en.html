About Us

Kangwon National University Game Development Club Potanity
POTATO + UNITY

Potanity is a central game development club at Kangwon National University,
where students from various fields such as game planning, programming, graphics, and sound gather
to create creative and innovative games together.

We enhance our skills through Unity-based projects,
regular study sessions, monthly meetings, planning studies, participation in game exhibitions, game jams, and other activities.
Anyone with a passion for game development is welcome!

Potanity Mini-Game Showcase

Check out the mini-games developed by Potanity!

Mini-game 1: Metal Slug

An action shooting game that pays homage to the classic Metal Slug. Clear various weapons and stages while defeating enemies!

Mini-game 2: Reaction Speed Test Game

Measure your reaction speed by clicking as quickly as possible when a signal appears on the screen! Challenge your reflexes.

Mini-game 3: Watermelon Game (Under Development)

A puzzle game where you stack fruits to create a watermelon! Strategically stack fruits to challenge the highest score.

Mini-game 4: Pico Park (Under Development)

A multiplayer puzzle game where cooperation is key! Work with friends to clear stages.

Mini-game 5: Motion Recognition (Under Development)

A motion recognition game using a webcam! Move your body to complete various missions.

Mini-game 6: Sorting Game (Under Development)

A game where you quickly sort given objects into the correct categories! Test your speed and concentration.

Mini-game 7: Desert Survivor (Under Development)

A survival game where you survive in a hot desert! Manage limited resources and survive as long as possible.
